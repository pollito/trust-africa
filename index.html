Jackpot Lottery – How One Company Hides Its Billions

Written by Khadija Sharife, edited/byline by Jeff Lowenstein
World Policy Journal @trustafrica #Gamingthelottery #IFF

Gtech engaged in corporate inversion with IGT to slash its tax rate from 35% to 18%; holds $2b offshore, avoids $600m in jurisdictions where real economic activity occurs.

  • Exposes technique of corporate inversion used by Gtech during merger with IGT that slashed tax rates in half (35% to 18%)
  • Exposes $700 million in avoided tax annually where real economic activity takes place
  • Exposes offshore earnings of $2 billion
  • Exposes how this happened through Cyprus-based holding entity that received “fees” from subsidiaries located in real countries like SA, India, etc. Revenue of $200m with earnings of $600 million withheld.
